Fun Facts about Annie Easley

1. Annie Easley was the president of the NASA ski club

2. She began to work at NACA's Lewis Research Center after hearng about twins sisters who worked as human computers for NACA.

3. Annie worked for NASA for 34 years. She retired in 1989 when she was 56.

4. Later in her career, Annie starting working as an equal employment opportunity counselor. She helps supervisors adress discrimination complaints in the most colaborative way possible.

5. She and her supervisor paved the way for women's rights at the research center, when they made a pact to wear pant suits together. She said, it did cause quite a stir. We took the emphasis off of what you’re wearing. It’s more like what you’re actually producing

6. She traveled to cape canaveral to watch the rocket launches
